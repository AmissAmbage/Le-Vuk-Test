    What sets training frequency?

    Training frequency is driven by recovery capacity, technical skill, and the size of your calorie surplus or deficit. Deeper deficits call for fewer hard sets per muscle, while lean bulks thrive on higher frequency so long as sleep and stress are well-managed.
